Privacy Policy

Slade Holdings LLC operates a private QuickBooks connection service for companies managed or expressly authorized by Slade Holdings.

Information processed

With authorization, the service may access accounting information available through the QuickBooks Online Accounting API, including company, account, transaction, customer, vendor, invoice, bill, and reporting data. It also retains the QuickBooks company identifier and authorization credentials needed to maintain the connection. QuickBooks Payments permission is not requested.

Use of information

Information is used only for internal accounting, reporting, reconciliation, financial administration, and authorized automation. Slade Holdings does not sell QuickBooks data or use it for advertising.

Security and retention

Connection credentials are protected and access is limited to authorized administrators. Information is retained only as long as reasonably needed for the described purposes, security, and applicable recordkeeping obligations.

Sharing

Information may be disclosed to service providers supporting the integration, when directed by an authorized company representative, or when required by law.

Choices

An authorized administrator may revoke access through QuickBooks Online. Requests about a connection may be directed through the company administrator who provided authorization.
